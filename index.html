Saeed Iqbal, Ph.D.

Research Associate Professor

I am a Research Associate Professor in the College of Mechatronics and Control Engineering at Shenzhen University, China. Previously, I served as an Assistant Professor at the University of Central Punjab (UCP), Pakistan.

My research focuses on Federated Learning, Continual Learning, Machine Unlearning, and the computer-aided analysis of medical images. I aim to develop privacy-preserving, robust AI solutions that enhance diagnosis and prognosis in healthcare, bridging the gap between computational methods and clinical applications.
